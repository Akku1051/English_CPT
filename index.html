Intro



Welcome to my website on the internal conflicts found within Macbeth, Indian Horse, and Two Kinds. Internal Conflicts are battles that are fought within the human psyche such as Macbeth’s guilt, Saul’s trauma, or Jing Mei’s feeling of being overwhelmed. Each of these characters fights through these emotions through their stories and their abilities to cope with their internal conflicts defines their strength as a character
